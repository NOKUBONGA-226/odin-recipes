Malva Pudding

Description

Malva pudding is a nice South African dessert with a soft texture and it has sweet flavour.

Ingredients

The Pudding

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp apricot jam
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda

For the sauce

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp butter

Steps

Pudding

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Ovens are temperamental, so if you know yours needs a bit more (or less) of a push, feel free to adjust.
  2. In a large mixing bowl add your egg, sugar, vanilla essence and jam. Whisk until smooth.
  3. To your mixture add your cup of milk, vinegar and your melted butter. Give it another stir to mix all of your wet ingredients.
  4. In another measuring bowl, add flour, bicarbonate of soda and salt and give it a quick stir.
  5. Add your dry ingredient to your wet ingredients and mix well as you go along.
  6. Pour your mixture into a lightly greased baking pan.
  7. Bake for 30 - 45 minutes. The pudding will stay in its mold and rest for a bit once the sauce is poured over.

Sauce

  1. Add all sauce ingredients together in a pot and bring to boil.
  2. Let it simmer, stirring constantly, for 3 minutes. This time will give the sauce a richer, deeper taste.
  3. Poke hole in a malva pudding so the sauce can soak in. Then pour the sauce over the pudding making sure you not leaving any part dry
