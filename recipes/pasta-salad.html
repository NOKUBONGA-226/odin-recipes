Pasta Salad

Description

This pasta salad with pepperoni and cheese is the best. It's a very easy, light-tasting side dish for a picnic or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 package spiral pasta
  • 3 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salami, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 can black olives, drained
  • 1 jar pimentos, drained
  • 1 bottle italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spiral pasta in the boiling water, stirring ocassionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Combine pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pasta, olives and pimentos in a large bowl.
  4. pour salad dressing, toss to coat.
  5. Enjoy!
